Who we are?

NewBoldBlock is an animation studio that focus on creating relatable and fun video content. Cofounded by Adebayo Yomi and Adebayo Nisola.

Adebayo Yomi

Yomi has been in the animation craft since 2015, He's had the opportunity to work with corporate and tech companies. He's a mouse potato, no one touches his PC except him. Also a deadline junkie, never failed at that, trust us we've seen him try.

Adebayo Nisola

Nisola is an illustration artist with 5 years experience. Hates lazy work, believes in the process. If things work out well He should progress into movie directing. A good movie is his muse. His famous words for work ethic: No pain!!! No gain!!!
